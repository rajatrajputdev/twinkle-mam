about The Dance Teacher

Twinkle Sharma, She has been teaching dance since 2008

In a world where women often face societal pressures to conform to traditional roles, the inspiring journey of Miss Twinkle serves as a powerful testament to resilience and determination. Born and raised in Saharanpur, India, Twinkle began her dance training at the tender age of five under the guidance of her maternal grandmother. Over the next six years, she honed her skills, ultimately performing her first stage dance at the age of six. This early success set the stage for a remarkable career, culminating in a performance before Russian President Mikhail Gorbachev at the cultural house of the Russian embassy when she was eighteen. Twinkle's talent did not go unnoticed; she quickly became a celebrated figure in her hometown(Saharanpur), gaining recognition through numerous newspaper features and television appearances. Her prowess in dance earned her accolades, including the prestigious Nritya Guruvarya Awards and the Top 100 Women Icon Award. However, her life took a dramatic turn when she married at twenty-one, coinciding with her with household chores . In the midst of family obligations, she paused her artistic pursuits to care for him, temporarily sidelining her dreams. Following her household responsibilities, Twinkle faced a pivotal moment. Rather than allowing her ambitions to remain dormant, she resolved to revive her passion for dance. Demonstrating remarkable entrepreneurial spirit, she established two dance academies—"Shri Natraj Nritya Academy"—in different cities, where she began teaching Kathak to aspiring dancers. Twinkle's dedication to her craft and her commitment to empowering women became a driving force in her life. She emerged as a role model, challenging the patriarchal norms that dictate women's roles in society. Twinkle's story highlights the theme of perseverance in the face of adversity. Despite the societal expectation for women to prioritize household responsibilities after marriage, she proved that it is possible to pursue one's passions and achieve professional success. Her journey is a vivid illustration of how women can balance multiple roles while continuing to strive for their dreams. Twinkle's achievements resonate beyond her personal success; they serve as an inspiration for countless women who may feel constrained by societal norms. Her narrative underscores the importance of self-belief and hard work, illustrating that dreams can be fulfilled regardless of life's challenges. As she continues to mentor young dancers, Twinkle not only preserves the art of dance but also fosters a new generation of empowered women ready to break free from societal limitations. In conclusion, Miss Twinkle's life story is a compelling narrative of hope, resilience, and triumph over adversity, emphasizing that with determination and courage, anyone can overcome obstacles and achieve their dreams, regardless of the circumstances they face."

about her guiding light

Pandit Govardhan Maharaj, has been the first Guru and ultimate Guiding Light

My journey into the mesmerizing world of Indian classical dance began with a stroke of divine fortune under the profound guidance of my first guru, the illustrious Pandit Govardhan Maharaj of Haridwar. Renowned across the nation as a master of Kathak, his presence radiates the essence of tradition, discipline, and boundless creativity. His teachings went far beyond the rhythmic patterns and intricate footwork; they delved deep into the soul of the art form, unveiling its spiritual and emotional depths. Pandit Govardhan Maharaj approached every lesson with unmatched passion and precision, leaving an indelible mark on my heart and mind. His gentle yet firm guidance taught me that Kathak is not merely a dance but a dialogue—a profound conversation between the dancer, the divine, and the audience. Under his watchful eyes, I learned to weave stories through movement, to balance grace with power, and to harmonize discipline with the spontaneity that breathes life into every performance. It is impossible to overstate the impact of his mentorship on my journey. Every spin, every beat, and every gesture I execute carries the imprint of his wisdom. His teachings have not only shaped my skills but enriched my soul, providing me with a lens through which to view life itself—as a rhythm, a melody, and a story waiting to unfold. I am eternally indebted to Pandit Govardhan Maharaj for sharing this priceless legacy with me. His influence continues to illuminate my path, inspiring me to honor the tradition he so lovingly imparted and to ensure that the flame of Kathak remains vibrant for future generations. It is my life's mission to share this art with the same passion, precision, and reverence that he bestowed upon me.

about her guiding light

Geetanjali Lal, has been the guiding Guru Ma

At the heart of my journey in Indian classical dance lies the profound mentorship of my Guru Maa, Shrimati Geetanjali Lal, a renowned Kathak maestro celebrated worldwide for her contributions to the art form. As one of the leading exponents of the Jaipur Gharana of Kathak, her dedication to preserving and innovating this timeless tradition is unparalleled. Her expertise in abhinaya, intricate rhythmic footwork, and emotive storytelling has left an indelible mark on my artistic growth. Shrimati Geetanjali Lal’s illustrious career spans decades, enriched by her training under legendary figures such as Pandit Gopi Krishna and Roshan Kumari. She has held esteemed positions, including Chief of Repertory and Director at Kathak Kendra, and is a top-grade artist with Doordarshan and ICCR. Her achievements include the prestigious Sangeet Natak Akademi Award, as well as numerous other accolades such as “Nritya Sharada,” “Bharat Gaurav,” and “Kalpana Chawla Award.” Her choreographic brilliance has been showcased globally, blending traditional themes with contemporary narratives. Under her guidance, I have had the privilege of honing my skills through workshops and performances across Delhi and various other regions in India. These experiences have profoundly shaped my artistic journey, instilling discipline, creativity, and a deep reverence for Kathak. Guru Maa’s influence has not only elevated my craft but also enriched my understanding of the cultural and spiritual essence of this dance form. It is with heartfelt gratitude that I dedicate this platform to honoring her mentorship and her relentless efforts in keeping the flame of Kathak alive for generations to come. Her teachings inspire me every day to strive for excellence and share the beauty of Indian classical dance with the world.

about her guiding light

Workshop under Kathak Majestro Shrimati Vidha Lal Ji

The art of Kathak is a timeless tradition, blending storytelling, rhythm, and spiritual expression. My journey into this exquisite world was profoundly shaped by the guidance of Shrimati Vidha Lal, an iconic figure in Indian classical dance. Known for her extraordinary talent and dedication to preserving the Jaipur Gharana, Shrimati Vidha Lal is celebrated for her impeccable technique, innovative choreographies, and global contributions to Kathak. As an empanelled artist of the Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) and an ‘A’ grade performer for Doordarshan, Shrimati Vidha Lal has showcased Kathak on some of the most prestigious stages worldwide. Her performances at the Khajuraho Dance Festival, Kala Ghoda Arts Festival, and international events in Paris, New York, Vienna, and beyond have left audiences spellbound. She has earned countless accolades, including the Sri Krishna Gana Sabha Endowment Award, and set a Guinness World Record with an astonishing 103 spins in one minute—a testament to her technical mastery and creative spirit. Under her mentorship, I, Twinkle Sharma, have had the privilege of participating in multiple workshops across Delhi and other cities in India. Each session was a revelation, deepening my understanding of the intricate rhythms, graceful movements, and emotive storytelling that define Kathak. Shrimati Vidha Lal’s personalized teaching approach nurtured not just my skills but also my connection to the art’s rich heritage. Her teachings go beyond the steps and spins of dance; they are imbued with life lessons about discipline, dedication, and artistic integrity. Her ability to blend traditional techniques with contemporary perspectives inspired me to explore new horizons while staying rooted in the classical ethos. From Delhi’s vibrant cultural hubs to serene workshops in other cities, every moment under her guidance was transformative, instilling in me a profound respect for this ancient art form. Shrimati Vidha Lal’s illustrious career is marked by her pioneering spirit and relentless passion for Kathak. Her international tours have brought Indian classical dance to audiences across France, Germany, the USA, Canada, and Singapore, among many other countries, spreading the essence of Kathak to a global audience. She has performed for esteemed occasions, including a special choreography at Rashtrapati Bhavan in honor of foreign dignitaries and celebrations marking the 150th birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore. It is with immense gratitude and admiration that I dedicate this platform to honor Shrimati Vidha Lal’s invaluable contributions to my artistic journey and the larger realm of Indian classical dance. Her mentorship has not only shaped my craft but has also instilled a lifelong commitment to upholding and promoting the beauty of Kathak.
